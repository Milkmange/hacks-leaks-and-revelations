(U//FOUO ) While open-source reports suggest individuals at lawful protests are part of efforts associated with various extremist groups, extremist movements, or violent gangs, the majority of the violence and vandalism appears to be perpetrated by opportunistic, individual actors acting without specific direction.

 (U//FOUO ) The FBI, in close coordination with federal, state, and local partners, continues to monitor and respond to events related to the violence associated with lawful protests. The FBI continues to aggressively seek to corroborate any organized effort to incite violence by either known criminal groups or domestic violent extremists.
